# Design Decisions

## Why ChromaDB?

Chosen because:
- Open source
- Local execution
- Easy setup

## Why Qwen2.5:3B?

Chosen because:
- Runs efficiently on RTX 3060 6GB
- Good reasoning quality
- Local inference

## Why Sentence Transformers?

Chosen because:
- Fast
- Lightweight
- Industry standard for RAG prototypes
